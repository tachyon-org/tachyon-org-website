Personnel

The Tachyon Resilient Modeling project brings together investigators, researchers, students, and external collaborators across institutions and national laboratories. The project is intended to combine real world datasets from operations of the Dept. of Energy's leadership computing facilities, with advanced modeling techniques to create predictive models that can be used to increase the resiliency and fault tolerance of scientific workflows.

The Tachyon project is lead by Rensellear Polytechnical Institute in collaboration with Fermi national Accelerator Laboratory and Argonne National Laboratory. We have collaborators from the University of California Davis as well as University Illinios, Chicago.
